Julie Edwards


Home Town: San Fernando Valley.


Instruments: Drums and Vocals.


Background: Originally a member of the school choir where she met M and soon formed the band The Pity Party. The Pity Party recorded their first music on an 8-track recorder in Julie's studio apartment in Silverlake, California. Thier first self release was the The EP in 2006 where they folded and glued 750 CD sleeves made out of recycled Cereal boxes. Thier second self-release was Orgy Porgy which this time used recycled billboard vinyl to make the cd jackets. Some time later Julie was teaching in her shop when Lyndsey came in for a crochet lesson and Deap Vally was formed shortly after.

Lyndsey Troy


Home Town: San Diego.


Instruments: Vocals and Guitar.


Influences: Elliott Smith, the Shins and the Beatles amongst many others.


Background:Background: Lyndsey grew up in a very musical family, a fellow musician for a sister, songwriter father and a mother who always sang. Lyndsey and her sister Anna grew up playing music together in a band called The Troys. They got signed to Elektra and recorded an album that was never released. Lyndsey and her sister went their own separate ways musically with Lyndsey moving to L.A.
